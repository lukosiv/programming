About Body Mass Index (BMI)

Body mass index (BMI) is a value derived from a person's weight and height. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height and is universally expressed in units of kg/mÂ², resulting from mass in kilograms and height in meters.

It is used as a simple and quick way to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight.
